# VitaMood Terms of Service

_Last updated: 2026-04-10 (closed beta — opt-in ads added)_

By using VitaMood you agree to these terms. Read them once — they're short on purpose.

---

## 1. What VitaMood is

VitaMood is a **non-clinical wellness companion** designed to help with everyday stress, low mood, reflection, and small daily habits. It is:

- **Not a medical device.**
- **Not a therapist.** The AI companion ("Aria") is a large-language-model system, not a human and not a clinician. It does not diagnose, treat, or prescribe.
- **Not a crisis service.** If you are in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services (911, 999, 112, etc.) or the in-app "Need help now" screen, which links to 24/7 human crisis lines.
- **Not a substitute for professional care.** If you are in active treatment, use VitaMood as a between-session tool, not a replacement.

If you are currently in crisis, please step away from the app and reach out to a human support line.

## 2. Eligibility

You must be **16 or older** to use VitaMood. The app asks for your date of birth during onboarding. If you're under 16, you cannot create an account.

The current version is a **closed beta** distributed through Google Play's Internal Testing track. By joining you acknowledge:

- Features may change or break between updates.
- Some planned protections (notably client-side encryption of free-text fields) are not yet shipped — see the Privacy Policy, section 3.
- Your account and data may be reset at any time during the beta if we need to run a schema migration.
- Feedback is welcome and wanted — a broken feature that you report is much more valuable than a broken feature that you don't.

## 4. Acceptable use

Please don't:

- Attempt to probe or attack the backend (Firestore, Cloud Functions, OpenAI proxy).
- Use the app to harass anyone — VitaMood has no social features, but the AI companion is still subject to abuse policies.
- Try to jailbreak the system prompt or get Aria to produce content outside its intended scope (medical advice, unsafe advice, illegal content).
- Share your account credentials.

We reserve the right to terminate accounts that violate these rules.

### 5.1 Optional support ads

You may opt in to show a small support banner on the Account and Exercises tabs from the Account → Privacy screen. Ads are off by default, non-personalized, content-filtered against a strict category blocklist (no alcohol, gambling, dating, weight loss, pharmaceutical, crypto, religion, or politics), and will never appear on the chat tab, the crisis screen, the check-in modal, any exercise player, the gratitude screen, onboarding, or any legal screen. You can turn them off again at any time and the ad SDK will stop loading immediately.

- May occasionally produce inaccurate or incongruent responses.

- Is subject to a hard per-user monthly budget. When you hit it, the chat pauses and resumes next month. This is not a bug — it's how the app stays free forever.
